What is MjengoAI?

MjengoAI is an intelligent digital platform designed to simplify the entire building journey by connecting homeowners, developers, professionals, artisans, suppliers, contractors, financiers, and regulators in one integrated ecosystem. The name “Mjengo” is the Swahili language word for construction or building, making MjengoAI a distinctly African technology solution focused on transforming how people plan, build, and manage construction projects. — anywhere in Kenya.

Whether you are building a home in Turkana, renovating in Nairobi, or sourcing precast slabs in Mombasa, MjengoAI puts every construction contact you need at your fingertips.

Our Mission

To make Kenya's construction industry transparent, accessible and trustworthy — by giving every professional a verified digital profile and every client the tools to find, compare and hire with confidence.
