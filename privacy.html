Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Effective Date: 13 May 2026  ·  Version 1.0  ·  Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 Compliant

1. Our Commitment

MjengoAI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Full name and professional title
  • Mobile phone number (your unique account identifier)
  • Email address, location, county and town
  • Professional qualifications and NCA/EBK/AAK registration numbers
  • Profile photo and portfolio images
  • Pricing, availability and about information

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited and features used

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and display your public listing on MjengoAI
  • To verify your identity via phone OTP (SMS)
  • To send SMS notifications about your account and enquiries
  • To process ratings and reviews of your services
  • To improve the platform through anonymised analytics
  • To comply with Kenyan legal obligations

4. Dormant Contacts

Some contacts were initially listed from our founders' professional network. If you are listed and did not explicitly register, you have the right to claim your profile, correct any information, or request complete removal. Email us at info@mjengoai.com — all removal requests are processed within 72 hours.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Clients using the platform who view your public profile
  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and data storage
  • Law enforcement when required by Kenyan law
  • Professional bodies (NCA, AAK, EBK) for credential verification

6. Your Rights (Kenya DPA 2019)

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — Limit how we process your data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — Object to marketing communications at any time

To exercise any right, email: info@mjengoai.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase Firestore security rules restrict unauthorised access
  • Phone OTP prevents unauthorised account access
  • Regular security reviews of platform code and rules

8. Data Retention

  • Active member profiles — retained for duration of membership plus 2 years
  • Dormant profiles — retained until removal is requested
  • Analytics data — retained for 24 months in anonymised form

9. Cookies

MjengoAI uses minimal local storage for session preferences (location filters, dismissed banners) and service worker cache for offline functionality. No third-party advertising cookies. No cross-site tracking.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have violated your data protection rights, contact:

  • MjengoAI: info@mjengoai.com
  • Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya: www.odpc.go.ke
